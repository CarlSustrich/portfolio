About Me

Hello, I'm Carl, a student full stack developer! I've previously obtained a Bachelors of Science in Kinesiology, the study of the human body, how it moves and quantifying it. After having worked a variety of jobs ranging from a personal trainer, to a mortgage processor, to an arborist, I found myself walking the road toward a tech career. While I don't yet know whether I'll find myself trending toward front end or back end development, I'm here to learn everything in between! I'm eager to find a career that allows me to exercise my brain, rather than my body, as in my free time I enjoy rock climbing, mountaineering and mountain biking!
